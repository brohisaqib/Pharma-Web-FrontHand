    MEDCATIONS


    In order to avoid any kind of medication errors and to make our distribution process more efficient, we have situated our pharmacies within hospital floors and wards. For efficiency and effectiveness, we have initiated a number of different practices such as our computerized physician order entry system (CPOE), modified unit dose drug distribution system and therapeutic drug monitoring (TDM) to ensure medications delivered to our patients in the hospital wards are safe, correct and are of the right dosage.
